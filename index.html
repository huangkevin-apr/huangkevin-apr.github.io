About Me

Huang Kai — Munich / Singapore

I am HUANG Kai, a 2nd-year doctoral candidate at the Technical University of Munich (TUM), advised by Prof. Dr. Chunyang Chen. I am also a research engineer at SMU/NTU Singapore, advised by Prof. Yang Liu, Prof. Xiaofei Xie, and Prof. Yue Duan.

My research interest lies in large language models for automated program repair (LLM4APR), with a particular emphasis on multimodal bug scenarios such as front-end development and visualization-based interactions.

I have published 6 papers (5 first author, 1 co-first author) in top conferences and journals including ICSE, ASE, TSE, and CSUR, and received two ACM SIGSOFT Distinguished Paper Awards (ASE 2023 and ASE 2025).

Recently, I have been working on multimodal software engineering.

👉 I am actively seeking opportunities for research collaboration and providing BS/MS thesis mentoring for TUM/NTU students. If you are interested in my research, I would be very glad to hear from you at kai-kevin.huang@tum.de.
